Running more doesn't necessarily mean a good year of running

Last year I ran the most I'd ever run. This year I ran 50% more than last year! The main reason was signing up to my first ever ultra with a couple of friends. We ran round the Isle of Wight (106km) one Saturday in May. Lots of ways to train for long races but I went for the option of running more but at lower intensity. Fitted this into my week mainly by doing a few more commute runs and slightly longer weekend runs. Also while taking some time off after big runs I made sure I got back into at least low level training quicker than previous years. Am getting older and find having an extended fallow period makes getting back into training pretty tough.

Loved the ultra and have really loved the trail running community. Post IOW ran two more ultras (50k in Tring in summer and 50k in Surrey hills in Dec). In addition ran the London marathon as part of my training and did Hackney half with techrunners (and suprised myself with my fastest time on that course despite lack of speed training). Techrunners I stepped back in the summer - being the main person responsible for organising the weekly runs is quite tough over a long time and I needed a breather.

Next year I'm moving jobs and will have a super short commute. Whilst I can definitely make the run home longer it's hard to push and do those extra KM on days when you are tired. I expect next year to run less but am planning on doing more high quality runs - hoping to join some track and hill training sessions. Main aim of the year is to run a London marathon qualifying time in Milton Keynes marathon in may and then run a couple of ultra trail runs.

Swimmign & Yoga

Next year planning on the same one and looking at some longer distance ones too.

After experimenting with self pracitce a previous year I'm back in the swing of classes - mainly at Hot Pod Yoga in hackney. Have made sure I had time to do the yoga to balance the extra running I do - generally when I run but don't do yoga I get injured - this year has been fine.

January I have a month off and am going to be completing my 200 hour yoga teacher training in Bali. Super excited as has been a goal of mine to do for a while now and luckily managed to organise time off between jobs to finally get to do it. Expect next years yoga numbers to be through the roof!
